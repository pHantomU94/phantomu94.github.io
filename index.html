📚 My Research

I am deeply interested in performance optimization for model training and inference systems. My current research focuses on building systems that support AI training and inference, such as distributed model training platforms, cross-hardware edge inference platforms, and integrated training–inference machines.

During my postgraduate studies, my research primarily centered on network communication optimization within large-scale data computing system (like Hadoop and Spark).
